About Pathshala

What We Do

We help craft an application that stands out to admission officers, increasing your chances of receiving an admission offer and/or scholarship in top institutions abroad.

Who You Should Be

  • Aspiring undergrad looking to study outside Bangladesh
  • Focused on applying to US institutions
  • Completing HSC or A Levels by August 2025
  • Gap year students are welcome

Who You Should NOT Be

If you're looking to go to any US university regardless of quality, or want us to write your essays for you, we’re not the right fit for you.

What's Included

We offer knowledge sessions, strategy consultations, essay reviews, visa help, and more, ensuring you are fully equipped for success.

How to Pay?

Payment Structure

The total fee for Pathshala is BDT 75,000, payable over three months:

  • First month: BDT 25,000
  • Second month: BDT 25,000
  • Third month: BDT 25,000

If you need more time to pay, we can accommodate installment plans based on your circumstances.

Financial Aid Program

We offer financial aid for students who cannot afford Pathshala’s fees. Aid is based on both need and merit, but our funds are limited, so only a select few will be eligible. To apply for financial aid, please complete the Financial Aid Form provided with the application.
